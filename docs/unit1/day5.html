Day 5 - Exam Review

Use the properties of equality to justify solving the following.

  1. Explain why the definition of division leads to an important caveat that will follow you throughout your mathematical careers.

  2. Given the operation \(\bigoplus\) defined as \(a \bigoplus b = a + b -2\), what is the identity under this operation? The inverse under this operation?

  3. \(3x+4=28\)

  4. \(4(x-7)+9=11-2(x+1)\)

  5. \(\frac{2}{3}(x+6)+ \frac{1}{6}x=\frac{1}{2}(4-x)\)

  6. \(a(bx+c)=dx\)

  7. \(\frac{4}{15}x+\frac{13}{20} =\frac{9}{10}-\frac{19}{30}\)

  8. Solve for \(m\), \(y=mx+b.\)

Simplify each of the following.

  1. \(\frac{x^{3}y^{4}}{x^{5}{y^{2}}}\)
  2. \(\sqrt[4]{\frac{x^{25}y^{24}}{x^{9}y^{8}}}\)
  3. \(\sqrt{90}\)
  4. \(\sqrt{32}\)
  5. \(\sqrt[3]{40}\)
